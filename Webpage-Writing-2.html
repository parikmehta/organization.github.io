Organization

By:Parik Mehta

Many people do things at home to protect, but these small things are meaningful,
but they don’t reach out as easily. Organizations can stand out a lot, and they
can come up on the news, tv, and many places. Organization is a way to help our
environment, from many problems, like habitat destruction, pollution, etc. Joining,
and supporting them can help the environment, and help spread awareness about these
cases. So join, and help people around you know more. Here is a list of organizations
that are striving to make the environment a better place.

1. Earthjustice

Mission: To change some laws that are hurting the environment, or spread new laws of using
less of one thing to save it from others.

This organization was founded in 1966. Since then, they have been supporting many acts like
the Clean Air Act, or the Endangered Species Act, which have turned out to be successful. Today,
Earthjustice is the biggest environmental law organization in the US. This group has the support
of activists, national politicians, and individuals. This organization would love to have your
support as well. You can support them by giving donations, or giving your voice in important
campaigns. For more information, visit this link: https://earthjustice.org/

2. Environmental Defense Fund (EDF)

Mission: To preserve natural habitats on which life depends on.

This organization is a non-profit organization, striving to find solutions to today’s environemnt’s
urgent challenge. Their solutions are practical, and sustainable for the group and organization. They
have support from scientists, economists, policy experts, and professionals around the world, and they
would love to have your support as well. Some ways you can support them is by making a donation, or
signing up to receive alerts and warnings about urgent campaigns and issues. If you would like to have
more information, visit this link: https://www.edf.org/

3. The Natural Resource Defense Council (NRDC)

Mission: To make earth a safer place for humans, animals, and plants.

This organization is a non-profit group that was founded in the 1970s, by students and attorneys. This
organization is a membership based organization, which requires you to be a member, for you to be a part
of this club. It collaborates with lawyers, activists, scientists, businesses, and individuals internationally.
This group has ensured clean water, clean air, and healthy wild species around the globe. To get involved,
become a member, and then donate some money, or speak up in urgent issues, and campaigns. Here is a link for
more information: https://www.nrdc.org/

4. American Forests

Mission: To conserve and protect US and Canadian forests.

This organization works with many different brands, and American and Canadian governments. They have been trying
to restore native trees and forest. In addition, they promote urban forests to many places. Furthermore, they change
bad forest laws, that harm the forest, or harm the being within them. One of their achievements is that they have
successfully planted 50 million trees within North America. Some ways you can get involved with this group is by
donating some money, or gifting a plant, which will eventually turn into a tree, to the organization. Here is a link
for more information: https://www.americanforests.org/

5. Conservation International (CI)

Mission: Their mission is to protect food, fresh water, livelihoods, and stable climates.

This organization, CI, is a non-profit group of people, who are protecting our food, and water. CI is an international
organization saving sources of food, water, and life. One of their greatest achievements is that they have protected
approximately 601 hectares of land, marine, and coastal areas. They have the support of government, business leaders, and
people. Some ways you can get involved is by donating, or signing up for notifications about call to action campaigns. Here
is a link for further information: https://www.conservation.org/
